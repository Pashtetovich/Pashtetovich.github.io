Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

How much time per week should I budget?
Expect 6–8 hours a week: one 90-minute live session, one 60-minute office hour, and the rest on lessons and your project. Most students work full-time jobs while taking the course.
Are the live sessions recorded?
Yes. Every session is recorded within 24 hours and uploaded to the course portal. Live attendance isn't required — we have students on every timezone from Auckland to San Francisco.
Do I need prior experience?
This is an intermediate-to-senior course. You should have at least 2 years of product design experience and be comfortable in Figma. If you're unsure, reply to the confirmation email and we'll help you figure out fit.
What's the refund policy?
Full refund any time before the end of week 2. Email us — no forms, no questions. After week 2, we'll work with you on credit toward a future cohort.
Will my employer reimburse this?
Most employers do. We provide a pre-written reimbursement request with ROI framing that's been approved at Google, Stripe, Microsoft, Shopify and dozens of smaller teams. Ask us and we'll send it.
Is there a certificate?
Yes — a signed certificate of completion, and a LinkedIn credential you can add to your profile. More useful in practice is the alumni network, which regularly gets members referral intros.
Do you help with job placement?
We're not a bootcamp — we won't guarantee a job. But we run an active internal job board, partner with hiring managers at 40+ companies, and 87% of alumni get a promotion or new role within 6 months.
What if I need a payment plan?
Pay in 3 interest-free monthly installments is available at checkout. For designers in lower-income regions or between roles, we also run a limited number of full scholarships each cohort — write to us.
