Our Story

A taste of the
Mediterranean coast

Olivea was born of long summers along the Ligurian shoreline — afternoons spent pulling herbs from the hillside, evenings around a table that never seemed to end. We brought that table with us.

Our kitchen is led by Chef Marco Laurenti, whose cooking honours old-world technique while staying quietly curious. Produce arrives daily from small farms and coastal waters; the menu shifts with the season, never the trend.

— Marco Laurenti
